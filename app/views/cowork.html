At LFG we have a work area specializing in the needs of digital artists and game designers although community welcomes anyone who can use a great place to connect to wifi, use some office supplies and get to work.

We are proud to offer access to a Cintiq 27" tablet to all monthly members. We put a 2 hour a day limit on reservation time but you may use it as long as there isn't another person waiting for it.

LFG offers multiple server tools to help developers test and work on their games. We offer the Unity Master Server at our location for testing purposes, along with a Unity Assets server and VPN access to the cowork network to monthly members in case they need to offer outside contractors access to their machines.
